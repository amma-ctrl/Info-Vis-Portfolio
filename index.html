FINAL PORTFOLIO

Welcome to my information visualization portfolio! This collection showcases my growth as a designer and data storyteller, featuring projects that have been thoughtfully reworked based on user research and feedback. From exploring representation in the Smithsonian’s open access collections to diving deep into the Degrees & Disparities project, highlighting the gap between Black women’s graduate degree attainment and leadership roles, I’ve aimed to make complex issues both engaging and accessible. I wrap it all up with a self-assessment reflecting on what I’ve learned, how I’ve grown, and where I’m headed next.
